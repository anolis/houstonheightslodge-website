Eyeglass Collection Drive

We're collecting donated eyeglasses to help people in need. If you have old glasses sitting in a drawer, bring them along to any lodge night — drop them off and we'll make sure they find someone who needs them.

Lodge nights are every Tuesday at 7:30 PM at 115 E. 14th St., Houston, TX 77008.

Monthly Meet and Greet

The public is invited to join us on the first Tuesday of each month at 7:30pm to mingle, enjoy snacks and beverages and find out more about us.
